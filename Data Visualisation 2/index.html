Afghanistan the unhappiest country?

Afghanistan, a miserable country which has been devastated by war for the past few decades and is also known to be one of the most corrupted countries in the world. This visulisation aims to explore the environmental factors that explains why Afghanistan is the unhappiest country.

Happiness score around the world

According to World Happiness Report 2020, Afghanistan appears to be the unhappiest country among the 153 countries in the survey. This can be seen from the world map where Afghanistan has the most green colour compared to its neighbouring countries. The following visualisations will explore the reason behind this.



What is the cause?

The World Happiness Report 2020 stated that:

  • Corruption uses a scale of 0 to 100, 0 being very clean and 100 being highly corrupt.

  • Freedom to make life choices also uses a scale of 0 to 100, 0 being totally restricted and 100 being totally free.

    • Among the 153 countries, Afghanistan appears to be the most corrupted country in the world in terms of their government and business. Afghan traditions of patronage have resulted in a constant tampering of merit-based appointments to various positions relating to political influences which has resulted in massive corruption. In addition, Afghanistan is also ranked last when it comes to freedom. This is mainly due to their culture and religion that limits one's choices and the current ongoing war also plays a huge part in this.
